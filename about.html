Our Story

About The Ridleys

Telling stories through songs since 2016

Who We Are

The Band

The Ridleys is a Filipino indie/alternative folk band based in Manila, Philippines. Formed in 2016, the band creates heartfelt melodies that resonate deeply with listeners across the nation and beyond.

The band is composed of Benny Manaligod (vocals, acoustic guitar), Jan De Vera (lead guitar, backup vocals), and Bryant Ayes (drums), with Modi Florentino as band manager. They are officially signed to independent label Underdog Music PH.

Known for their ability to tell stories through their music, each of The Ridleys' songs is a carefully crafted narrative, exploring the complexities of human emotion and experience. Their sound combines indie sensibilities with catchy melodies to produce music that is both approachable and authentic.

Timeline

Our Journey

2016
The Beginning

The band's origins trace back to 2016 when they finished recording their debut EP Aphrodite, released in March of the same year. This marked the beginning of a musical journey that would take them from intimate venues to sold-out concerts.

2019
Debut Album

The band released its debut album, Reflections On Moonlight and Poetry, featuring a mix of new recordings and several songs from the Aphrodite EP. The album showcased their growth as songwriters and musicians.

2022-2023
Until I Reach The Sun

From 2022 to 2023, the band released Until I Reach The Sun, a two-volume EP project. During this period, they also released "Intertwine", a collaboration single with fellow band Over October.

2024
Sophomore Album & First Solo Concert

The band released its sophomore album All These And More, featuring songs like "Be With You". In October of the same year, The Ridleys held their first-ever solo concert, marking a major milestone in their career.

2025
Moving Forward as a Trio

On March 2, 2025, bassist Joric Canlas announced his departure from the band. Following the announcement, Manaligod stated the band will move forward as a trio, continuing their musical journey with the same passion and dedication.

Philosophy

Our Music

The Ridleys' music is characterized by sincere lyrics and energetic rhythms that captivate listeners with a powerful sense of emotion and honesty. Their songs explore themes of longing, love, faith, and the human experience, creating narratives that resonate deeply with their audience.

From their debut album's liner notes: "We thank the moon for the light it provides, yet the moon has no light of its own. Its light, as bright as it is, is but a reflection of one that shines even brighter." This poetic philosophy reflects the band's approach to songwriting—finding beauty in reflection and creating music that points to something greater.

With their infectious spirit and undeniable talent, The Ridleys have quickly established themselves as one of the most exciting and compelling acts in the Philippine music scene today, building a devoted following and receiving favorable reviews from critics and fans alike.
