About

The Story Behind The Music

The Ridleys is a Filipino indie/alternative folk band based in Manila, Philippines. Formed in 2016, the band creates soulful melodies that resonate with listeners across the nation.

Officially signed to Underdog Music PH, the band released their debut EP "Aphrodite" in March 2016, marking the beginning of their musical journey.
